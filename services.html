Girls Education Programs

Enabling a future of equality and empowerment, our Girls Education Programs focus on breaking down barriers that hinder access to education. We provide tailored initiatives to ensure girls receive quality education, nurturing a generation of confident and educated women poised to contribute meaningfully to society.

Special Education Services (Disabilities)

Inclusive and compassionate, our Special Education Services cater to individuals with disabilities. We tailor educational approaches to meet diverse needs, fostering an environment where every person, regardless of ability, can access education and develop essential skills for a fulfilling life.

Rural Education Programs

Bridging the gap between urban and rural educational opportunities, our Rural Education Programs extend access to quality learning in remote areas. By establishing educational initiatives in rural communities, we aim to empower individuals with knowledge, skills, and resources for a brighter future.

Orphanage Education Support

Committed to the well-being of every child, our Orphanage Education Support initiative provides educational resources, mentorship, and a supportive environment for orphans. We believe in nurturing their potential, ensuring that they receive the education necessary for a successful and fulfilling life.

Empowering Women Through Education & Development

Our commitment to gender equality goes beyond classrooms. Through comprehensive programs, we empower women by providing education that extends beyond academics, encompassing personal development and skill-building. Our aim is to create self-reliant and empowered women who play vital roles in shaping their communities.

About Us

RYWS is recognised as one of the most trusted NGOs in Rajsthan. Through its projects spread across state, RWYS works on the issues of: child education, health & nutrition, child labour and child marriage. RWYS NGO addresses the critical needs of children by working with parents, teachers, Anganwadi workers, communities, district and state-level governments as well as the children themselves to create a sustainable change.
